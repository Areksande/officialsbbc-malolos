STATEMENT OF FAITH

I. THE BIBLE
We believe that the Bible is the verbal and plenary inspired preserved Word of God

II. THE PERSON OF GOD
We believe in God, the Supreme Personal Eternal Being, the source and beginning of all things, absolute in His attributes existing in Trinity.

III. THE PERSON OF JESUS CHRIST
We believe in the virgin birth of the Lord Jesus Christ, in His deity, His sinless life, and vicarious death and resurrection.
We believe in His pre-millenial return.

IV. THE HOLY SPIRIT
We believe in the Person of the Holy Spirit.
We believe that He indwells the believers and empowers them for service.

V. MAN
We believe that man was created by God in His image and by His own personal choice fell into sin and death and is under condemnation.

VI. SALVATION
We believe that salvation is wholly by grace and that the Lord Jesus Christ is the only way of salvation.

VII. THE SECURITY OF THE BELIEVERS
We believe that those who received the Lord Jesus Christ as personal Savior are eternally saved and the security of the believers rests upon the finished work of Christ on the Cross of Calvary.

VIII. HEAVEN AND HELL
We believe in the eternal blessedness of the saved in Heaven and the eternal punishment of the lost in Hell

IX. THE CHURCH
We believe that the New Testament Church is a congregation of baptized believers by immersion, practicing Biblical principles, holding forth its doctrines, observing its ordinances, exercising its autonomy and proclaiming Christ’s Gospel.
We believe that the Baptist church is the only kind of church the Lord Jesus Christ established.

X. SEPARATION
We believe in personal separation and ecclesiastical separation.
We take a stand against any trends in doctrine and practice which leads to descending the Bible and undermining the Fundamentals of Faith.
